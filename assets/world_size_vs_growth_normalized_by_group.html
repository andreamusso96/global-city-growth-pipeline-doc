world_size_vs_growth_normalized_by_group

Group: figure_data_prep

Description

We take the world_size_vs_growth_normalized table and we add a column to indicate the group of the city: 'above_1m', 'below_1m', or 'largest_city'. The 'largest_city' group is the city with the largest population in the country in the given year. We also exponentiate the population and growth columns to get the actual population and growth, so we can compute weighted average of growth by city population. Note that the groups are not mutually exclusive, so some cities may belong to multiple groups.
