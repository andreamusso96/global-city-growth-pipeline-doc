usa_cluster_base_matching

Group: usa_intermediate_create_clusters

Description

Matching between cluster geometries across different years. We take pairs of years and match clusters that intersect with each other spatially. Clusters that do not intersect with each other are matched to themselves. This yields a bipartite graph.

y2?The endpoint of the cluster growth
