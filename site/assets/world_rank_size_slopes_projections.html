world_rank_size_slopes_projections

Group: figure_data_prep

Description

The slopes of the log-rank vs log-size projected forward in time. The projection are based on an equation relating rank-size slopes and size-growth slopes: a_{t+10} = a_t * (1 + b_{t}) where a_t is the rank-size slope in year t and b_t is the size growth slope in year t.
